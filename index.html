Food is any substance consumed to provide nutritional support and energy to an organism. It can be raw, processed, or formulated and is consumed by animals for growth, health, or pleasure. Food is mainly composed of five food groups.

Vegetables

A vegetable is the edible portion of a plant. Vegetables are usually grouped according to the portion of the plant that is eaten such as leaves (lettuce), stem (celery), roots (carrot), tubers (potato), bulbs (onion) and flowers (broccoli).

Fruits

The sweet and fleshy product of a tree or other plant that contains seed and can be eaten as food.

Grains

Grains are the edible seeds of certain grasses. Wheat, oats, rice, and corn are common grain foods, as are barley, spelt, millet and sorghum.

Proteins

Protein is a nutrient your body needs to grow and repair cells, and to work properly. Protein is found in a wide range of food such as eggs, lentils, meat, and beans.

Dairy

Dairy products or milk products, also known as lacticinia, are food products made from (or containing) milk.
